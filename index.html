Dog Food

Description

This dogfood recipe offers a nutritionally balanced diet. It is an affordable alternative to "human grade" dog food, and it's easy, too!

  • 10 lbs of chicken
  • 5 cups of rice
  • 2 cups of veggies

It is important to note that some veggies are not safe for dog consumption. To learn more about which veggies your dog eat, check out This AKC list

Now that you have your ingredients ready, you're ready to begin making your pup's new favorite meal!

  1. Dump all ingredients into a crockpot
  2. Cook until done
  3. Chop, chop, chop! Your best friend may like big bites, mush, or something in between!
